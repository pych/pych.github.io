Pych

Pronounced "pitch"

Pythonists Chennai, a user group with Pythonic Kolaveri!

Contrary to status quo, Chennai can really have a kickass Python user group. Hence Pych!

 

 

Make Noise

We want to make a lot of noise about Python. Get the word across about its greatness, and effectiveness as a hacker tool.

Get Talking

We want to give Chennai Pythonists a place they could call home. Organise meetups, discussions, and hackathons to allow these folks to interact.

Love Chennai

We love Chennai. It has given us great coffee, beautiful coastline, and yummy dosas. Its hot. We want to give back something kickass.

Anyone with interest in Python and a slightest association with Chennai, can Pych

 

 

Backers

People want to come in to say, "Great job guys". And want to help, can Pych.

Guzzlers

People who have loads of Pythonic experience and kolaveri. And want to share that, can Pych.

Freshmen

People who just want to listen, and learn. And want to give back any possible way, can Pych.

Critics

People who wanna critic, or shoot us, for something that we do wrong. Can Pych.

We Love You for coming this far. It would nice to have you help us in sharing this joy. Pych in!

Join the Pych community on Google Plus. It would be fun to have you around.

Follow Pych on the twitter streams. We have loads of information to share.

We would love to meet you all soon. Its always fun to meetup and have chatting Pythons.
